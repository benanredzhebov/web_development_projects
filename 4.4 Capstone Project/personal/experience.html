My Professional Experience

I have a total of six years of experience in the glass production industry, where I worked at a leading European Glass Production Company. For the first five years, I served as a Production Planner. During this time, I was responsible for overseeing capacity planning, creating and maintaining production line schedules, managing packaging requirements, and coordinating raw materials between production departments. I also prepared monthly activity reports to evaluate performance and ensure efficient workflows.

After five years, I was promoted to the role of Production Engineer, where I played a key role in optimizing production processes by eliminating waste, managing resources, and implementing new technologies. I led efforts to streamline production by adopting the Just-in-Time strategy, which reduced overproduction and minimized downtime. One of my significant achievements was successfully managing color campaigns, which helped minimize thickness changeovers and reduced forced production. This initiative resulted in completing the expected production two days ahead of schedule.

In addition to production optimization, I managed inventory and coordinated with other departments to solve production challenges. A notable project involved resolving surface scratches on thin glass during production. My team and I conducted thorough analyses and determined that the issue stemmed from robotic handling and a specific section of the production line. By working with external experts for geodetic measurements and adjusting robotic programming, we successfully resolved the issue, ensuring smooth operations moving forward.

Throughout my time in the glass production industry, I gained valuable skills in team management, supply chain coordination, big data analysis, and reporting. These experiences helped me drive continuous improvement and optimize operations to meet production targets efficiently.
